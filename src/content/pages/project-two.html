Signs You Have a Clogged Pipe

  • Slow-draining sinks or bathtubs
  • Gurgling sounds from drains
  • Water backing up in sinks, toilets, or tubs
  • Unpleasant odors from drains
  • Frequent clogs requiring plunging

If you notice any of these issues, call Elzo Plumbing for a quick and effective fix!

Our Clogged Pipe Repair Process

Step 1: Inspection & Diagnosis

We use high-tech drain cameras and other diagnostic tools to pinpoint the clog’s location.

Step 2: Clearing the Blockage

Our team utilizes professional-grade drain snakes, hydro-jetting, or chemical treatments (if necessary) to safely remove the clog.

Step 3: Preventative Advice

We’ll provide expert tips to help prevent future clogs, saving you time and money.

Common Causes of Clogged Pipes

  • Grease & food debris buildup
  • Hair accumulation in bathroom drains
  • Tree roots invading sewer lines
  • Flushing non-flushable items (wipes, hygiene products)
  • Mineral deposits from hard water
