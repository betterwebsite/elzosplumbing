Signs Your Water Heater Needs Repair

A failing water heater can disrupt your daily routine. Watch for these warning signs:

  • No hot water or inconsistent temperatures
  • Strange noises (popping, banging, or hissing)
  • Water leaks around the heater
  • Rust-colored water from taps
  • A sudden spike in energy bills

If you notice any of these issues, call Elzo Plumbing for a quick and effective fix!
