About Us

YOUR TRUSTED PLUMBER FOR OVER 30 YEARS

Harry Elzo, owner of Elzo’s Plumbing Co. was born in Tulsa, and raised in Broken Arrow on 110 acres. Harry has more than 30 years of experience in plumbing. Harry’s mother was a nurse, and his father was an engineer and an entrepreneur who owned several successful businesses in the Tulsa area. Harry graduated from Broken Arrow High School, as well as Tulsa Vo-Tech. He also attended Oklahoma State University. After high school, Harry proudly served in the US Army (RAKKASAN!!). Harry also has a deep love for auto racing. “I’m fiercely, almost dangerously, loyal to my friends and family. I have a sweet wife, 4 intelligent, loving, daughters, 4 hardworking son-in laws and 8 wonderful grandchildren.” - Harry
